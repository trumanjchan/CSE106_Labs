About Us

Pokemon Simulator is a static website created by Truman Chan on 9/27/2021 for his Full Stack Development (CSE106) Lab03 assignment.

On PS, you will encounter Pokemon Trading Card Game content on the Home and Store page. The Home page displays a video of Pokemon TCG booster pack openings and a table of all the Pokemon Sword & Shield expansion release dates. The Store page shows a picture of a S&S booster pack. The Items and Pokemon pages display some Pokemon Go-related content.
